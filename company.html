OUR MISSION

Managing your finances is boring, hard work and often easy to ignore. Traditional banking and financial apps do not work for our generation.

Our mission is to make your money management just as fun, social and easy as using Instagram or Snapchat.

We're revolutionising the way you manage your money by creating an easy, social interface that helps you focus your saving efforts with the help of your friends. Squad puts you in control and arms you with the knowledge, drive and support to achieve your financial goals.
